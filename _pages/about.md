# Korean Migration And Politics Working Group

Welcome to **KMAP Working Group** (Korean Migration And Politics Working Group). We are an interdisciplinary research group dedicated to studying migration politics in and around Korea.

## Our Mission

KMAP Working Group brings together scholars, researchers, and students interested in understanding the political dimensions of migration in the Korean context. Our research covers:

- Immigration policy and governance in Korea
- Political participation of migrants
- Public attitudes toward immigration
- Comparative migration politics in East Asia
- Historical perspectives on Korean migration
